Mount Gozaisho

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset. Mount Gozaisho is a mountain nature spot in Komono, Mie. It works well for 自然, 缆车 and 红叶, with a typical visit time of 120-180 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
