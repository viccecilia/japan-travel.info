Kitano Ijinkan-gai

This is a stable, easy-to-understand and photogenic stop to add to a Kansai itinerary. Kitano Ijinkan-gai is a historic foreign-style district in Kobe, Hyogo. It works well for 建筑, 摄影 and 情侣, with a typical visit time of 60-120 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
