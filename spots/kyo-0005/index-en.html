Gion Hanamikoji Street

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset. Gion Hanamikoji Street is a traditional district in Kyoto City, Kyoto. It works well for 夜晚散步, traditional streets and photos, with a typical visit time of 45-90 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
