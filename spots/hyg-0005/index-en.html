Mount Rokko Night View

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset. Mount Rokko Night View is a night-view and nature spot in Kobe, Hyogo. It works well for night views, 情侣 and 摄影, with a typical visit time of 90-150 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
