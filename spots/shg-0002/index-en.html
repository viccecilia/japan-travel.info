Hikone Castle

This stop brings out the history and cultural depth of Kansai, beyond just taking photos. Hikone Castle is a castle and cultural site in Hikone, Shiga. It works well for 历史, castles and 樱花, with a typical visit time of 90-150 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
