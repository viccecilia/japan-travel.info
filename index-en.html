Kyoto & Nara World Heritage Classic Day Route

Spend the morning in Kyoto with Fushimi Inari, Kiyomizu-dera and Gion, then move to Nara for the deer park, Todai-ji and Kasuga Taisha. It is a strong first-time route for families, seniors and classic sightseeing travelers.

A great route for travelers who want coastal scenery, photography and a quieter side of Kansai. Amanohashidate gives the grand view; Ine adds the lived-in seaside atmosphere.

Lake Biwa, Shirahige Shrine & Omihachiman Day Route

A relaxed Shiga route with a lakeside torii, water town streets, castle scenery and open lake views. Great for photography, families and travelers who want fewer crowds.

Wakayama Cat Station & Kuroshio Market Family Day Route

Kishi Station and the cat stationmaster theme work well for families and relaxed photos, while Kuroshio Market gives the day a clear lunch and seafood experience. Wakayama Castle or a seaside walk can be added for more variety.
