Kobe, Arima Onsen & Mt. Rokko Night View Day Route

Start with Kobe Harborland, Kitano or Nunobiki Herb Garden, slow down at Arima Onsen in the afternoon, and finish with the night view from Mt. Rokko.

couplesfriendssenior travelersonsen and night views

Day Routes

Kobe does not win by being loud. Its charm grows as the day becomes more comfortable. Harborland opens the route with sea breeze and port scenery, Kitano adds a European-style atmosphere, and Nunobiki Herb Garden can create a gentle mountain-and-garden transition. In the afternoon, Arima Onsen shifts the day from sightseeing to relaxation. Mt. Rokko’s night view then gives the route a memorable final scene.

Relaxed scenic route, about 9–10 hours

1. Kobe Harborland

45–60 min A light start with sea breeze, harbor views and city lines.

2. Kitano Ijinkan Street

45–60 min One of Kobe’s best areas for a refined, international atmosphere.

3. Kobe Nunobiki Herb Garden

optional 60 min A good mountain-and-garden transition on clear days.

4. Arima Onsen

90–120 min The most relaxing part of the route.

5. Mt. Rokko night view

40–60 min A strong final memory for the day.
