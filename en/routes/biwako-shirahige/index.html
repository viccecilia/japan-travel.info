Lake Biwa, Shirahige Shrine & Omihachiman Day Route

A relaxed Shiga route with a lakeside torii, water town streets, castle scenery and open lake views. Great for photography, families and travelers who want fewer crowds.

KansaiKansaiKansaiKansai

Day Routes

Shiga is often underestimated, but it shows a quieter side of Kansai. Shirahige Shrine creates the opening image with its torii standing in the lake, while Lake Biwa brings a wide and calming view. Omihachiman slows the pace with canals and old streets, and Hikone Castle adds a layer of history. This route is not about intensity; it is about comfort, clean scenery and a slower rhythm.

Relaxed nature route, about 9–10 hours

1. Shirahige Shrine

40–50 min The torii in the lake is the signature image of this route.

2. Lake Biwa

30–45 min The open lake view naturally slows the day down.

3. Omihachiman

60–90 min A relaxed stop for canals, old streets and sweets.

4. Hikone Castle

45–60 min Adds history and castle scenery to a nature-focused day.
