05 · FAQ

Before you travel

FAQ →
Who operates Japan Travel?

Japan Travel is operated by Kabushiki Kaisha Daitora / Daitora Group. Public pages are travel references; transport services are fulfilled according to booking confirmation.

Are the vehicles legally operated in Japan?

Transport guidance assumes compliant local operation in Japan. Vehicle, driver and permit details are governed by booking confirmation, not by unconfirmed page claims.

How is this different from a normal taxi?

A taxi is best for immediate short rides. Japan Travel guidance is better for planned airport transfers, private routes, family travel and guest reception.

How is this different from airport buses or trains?

Buses and trains fit light luggage and fixed routes. Private transfer is useful for groups, luggage, children, seniors, late arrivals and hotel-to-hotel movement.
