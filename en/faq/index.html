FAQ

Who operates Japan Travel?

Japan Travel is operated by Kabushiki Kaisha Daitora / Daitora Group. Public pages are travel references; transport services are fulfilled according to booking confirmation.

Are the vehicles legally operated in Japan?

Transport guidance assumes compliant local operation in Japan. Vehicle, driver and permit details are governed by booking confirmation, not by unconfirmed page claims.

How is this different from a normal taxi?

A taxi is best for immediate short rides. Japan Travel guidance is better for planned airport transfers, private routes, family travel and guest reception.

How is this different from airport buses or trains?

Buses and trains fit light luggage and fixed routes. Private transfer is useful for groups, luggage, children, seniors, late arrivals and hotel-to-hotel movement.

Should I choose Alphard or Hiace?

Alphard fits smaller groups seeking comfort. Hiace is better for more passengers or luggage. Final assignment follows booking confirmation.

How do I choose by passenger and luggage count?

Enter passenger count, luggage count, child seats and large items. This helps judge vehicle type or whether multiple cars are needed.

How does airport pickup work?

Flight number, arrival date, party size, luggage and destination are confirmed first. Meeting details follow the booking confirmation.

What if I cannot find the driver?

Check the meeting instructions and contact method in your confirmation. If you still cannot meet, contact support through the email, LINE or inquiry channel.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Delay handling follows the Rezio product and final booking confirmation. Providing the flight number early helps coordination.

Can late-night arrivals be arranged?

Late-night service depends on date, vehicles and operating conditions. Page display before confirmation does not mean a booking is confirmed.

How is waiting time handled?

Waiting rules follow the Rezio product and final booking confirmation. Extra waiting may require new coordination.

Can child seats be prepared?

Child seats are special requests. Enter the requested number in advance. Availability follows booking confirmation.

What about seniors or children?

Share walking distance concerns, luggage, boarding ease and rest needs in advance so the route can be planned more comfortably.

Can multiple vehicles be arranged?

Multiple vehicles can be considered for larger groups or heavy luggage. Share passenger, luggage, pickup and drop-off details.

Is this suitable for corporate guests?

Yes, it can support airport pickup, guest reception and meeting transfers. Share timing, headcount, luggage and billing needs in advance.

Can hotels or travel agencies cooperate?

Use the inquiry form to describe cooperation style, guest language, frequency and settlement needs. Formal terms require Daitora Group confirmation.

What are cancellation and refund rules?

Cancellation, refund and change rules follow the relevant Rezio product and final booking confirmation.

How do I book through Rezio?

The Rezio button opens the relevant product or a friendly unconfigured page. Rezio handles dates, inventory, price, payment and vouchers.

What is the difference between inquiry and Rezio booking?

Inquiry is for complex routes and special needs. Rezio is for standard products, dates, inventory, payment and formal booking.

What happens after payment?

Order status after payment follows Rezio and final confirmation. A click on this site is only a redirect record, not a completed order.

How can I change booking information?

Follow Rezio order rules or the confirmation email. Whether changes are possible and any fee depend on final confirmation.

What are VIP benefits?

VIP can keep preferences such as language, drinks, temperature, past vehicles, family needs and welcome cards, with priority response during working hours.

What is not guaranteed by VIP?

VIP does not guarantee a specific driver, vehicle upgrade, unlimited waiting, free extra hours or free long-distance extra stops.

How is a referral code recorded?

Referral codes are random system values recorded on the server. The browser cannot edit referral relationships.

What is a valid referred order?

Only paid services satisfying valid-order rules count. Cancelled, refunded, test and self-referral orders do not count.

How are Instagram and TikTok sources identified?

UTM parameters, source channels and ref_code are preserved. Non-essential pixels do not load when not configured or not consented.

How is personal data protected?

Member data, bookings, referrals and tokens are not stored in localStorage. Server sessions, CSRF and token hashes are used.

What if luggage does not fit?

Enter luggage count and size accurately before booking. A larger vehicle or multiple cars may be suggested.

Can we add stops on the way?

Private charters can discuss extra stops, but feasibility depends on time, distance, parking and product rules.

Can private routes be adjusted?

Share desired spots and pace through inquiry. Suggestions are made according to time and traffic conditions.

What about Japanese holidays and peak seasons?

Peak seasons can tighten vehicle supply and crowd attractions. Book early and keep buffer time.

How do I book again after service?

Book again through Rezio or sign in to review favorites, preferences and referral records before sending a new inquiry.
