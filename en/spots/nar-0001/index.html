Nara Park

This is a good place to slow down and let Kansai shift from city to nature.

Kansaiparkwalk

This is a good place to slow down and let Kansai shift from city to nature. Nara Park is a family-friendly nature spot in Nara City, Nara. It works well for families, first-time Nara visitors and senior travelers, with a typical visit time of 90-150 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
