This is a good place to slow down and let Kansai shift from city to nature. Asuka Village is a ancient history and nature spot in Asuka, Nara. It works well for ancient history, 乡村 and 骑行, with a typical visit time of 半日. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
