Miho Museum

This is a good place to slow down and let Kansai shift from city to nature.

KansaiKansaiKansai

This is a good place to slow down and let Kansai shift from city to nature. Miho Museum is a art and architecture spot in Koka, Shiga. It works well for 建筑, 艺术 and 小众, with a typical visit time of 120-180 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
