Arima Onsen

This is where the route shifts from sightseeing into relaxation.

KansaiKansaiKansai

This is where the route shifts from sightseeing into relaxation. Arima Onsen is a hot spring area in Kobe, Hyogo. It works well for hot springs, senior travelers and 放松, with a typical visit time of 半日-住宿. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
