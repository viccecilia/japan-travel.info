Shima Spain Village

This stop is easy to understand, light to enjoy and especially friendly for families.

KansaiKansaiKansai

This stop is easy to understand, light to enjoy and especially friendly for families. Shima Spain Village is a theme park in Shima, Mie. It works well for families, theme-park fans and photos, with a typical visit time of 半日-全天. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
