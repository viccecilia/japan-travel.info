Abeno Harukas

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset.

KansaiKansaiKansai

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset. Abeno Harukas is a 城市观景 spot in Osaka City, Osaka. It works well for night views, shopping and rainy days, with a typical visit time of 60-90 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
