Day Routes

Kobe, Arima Onsen & Mt. Rokko Night View Day Route

Kobe does not win by being loud. Its charm grows as the day becomes more comfortable. Harborland opens the route with sea breeze and port scenery, Kitano adds a European-style atmosphere, and Nunobiki Herb Garden can create a gentle mountain-and-garden transition. In the afternoon, Arima Onsen shifts the day from sightseeing to relaxation. Mt. Rokko’s night view then gives the route a memorable final scene.

Use Harborland for wide port scenes, Kitano for portraits, Arima for onsen-town atmosphere, and Rokko for the final night view.

Kobe Port and Harborland

This stop opens up the view and works especially well on clear days or around sunset. Kobe Port and Harborland is a harbor area in Kobe, Hyogo. It works well for night views, shopping and families, with a typical visit time of 90-150 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
