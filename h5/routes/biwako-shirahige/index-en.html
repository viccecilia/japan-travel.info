Day Routes

Lake Biwa, Shirahige Shrine & Omihachiman Day Route

Shiga is often underestimated, but it shows a quieter side of Kansai. Shirahige Shrine creates the opening image with its torii standing in the lake, while Lake Biwa brings a wide and calming view. Omihachiman slows the pace with canals and old streets, and Hikone Castle adds a layer of history. This route is not about intensity; it is about comfort, clean scenery and a slower rhythm.

Shirahige Shrine

This stop adds sea breeze, lake views or a calm waterside pause to the route. Shirahige Shrine is a shrine with lake scenery in Takashima, Shiga. It works well for lake views, 摄影 and 神社, with a typical visit time of 45-75 min. For private-car planning, confirm the drop-off point, walking distance and meeting time in advance. When adding it to a private-car route, allow time not only for the stop itself, but also for photos, rest and the atmosphere.
